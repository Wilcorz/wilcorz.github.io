Ham Quiche’

 

Ingredients:

 

1/2 lb Mushrooms, sliced

2 Tablespoon Butter

4 Eggs

8 oz Sour Cream

1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese, Grated

1/4 Cup All-Purpose Flour

1 teaspoon Onion Powder

6 to 8 drops Hot Sauce

8 oz Monterey Jack Cheese, grated

1/2 Cup Cooked Ham, chopped

 

Step one:

1/2 lb Mushrooms, sliced

2 Tablespoon Butter

(Sauté Mushrooms in butter. Remove and drain on paper towels.)

 

Step Two:

4 Eggs

8 oz Sour Cream

1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese, Grated

1/4 Cup All-Purpose Flour

1 teaspoon Onion Powder

6 to 8 drops Hot Sauce

 

Combine the above in electric blender, process until well blended

 

Step Three:

8 oz Monterey Jack Cheese, grated

1/2 Cup Cooked Ham, chopped pat somewhat dry on paper towels.

 

In a bowel mix by hand the cheese, cooked ham, mushrooms and blended ingredients.

Pour into a greased 10 inch Quiche dish.

 

Step Four:

 

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Bake for 45 minutes. 

 

Cool 10 minutes before serving.
