Cold Pasta Salad

 

3 1/2 cups Ziti Rigati, Uncooked

3 cups chopped fresh or canned tomatoes

1/3 cup olive oil

1/4 cup finely chopped yellow onion (more if you wish)

2 Tablespoons balsamic vinegar

2 Tablespoons chopped fresh basil

2 Tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

2 Tablespoons finely chopped garlic ( get in a jar)

1/2 teaspoon salt

Pepper to taste

 

Cook pasta according to package directions; rinse in cold water and drain well.  In a large non-metal bowl, stir together all the ingredients (except the pasta).  Stir in pasta and chill for about 30 minutes.  Then serve.  This can be made the night before.
