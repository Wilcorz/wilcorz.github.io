Best Butter Cream Frosting Ever

1 Cup Butter, softened
1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
1/4 teaspoon salt (I just put a dash in)
4 cups Confectioners Sugar
1/2 cup seedless Blackberry Jam -- (Or ANY OTHER Jam that is seedless.)

Cream butter in mixer, add vanilla extract (blend well) add salt and start adding Confectioners Sugar 1/4 cup at a time and blending well after each 1/4 cup.
