Caviar Pie

Hands on time: 20 minutes  Total time: 6 hours and 30 minutes  Serves: 30

Ingredients:
6 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
3 tablespoons mayonnaise
1 1/2 cups sweet onion, finely chopped and drained on paper towel for 30 minutes
1 (8-ounce) package cream cheese, softened
2/3 cup sour cream
1 (3 1/2-4 ounce) jar Romanoff or other brand lumpfish caviar (red, black or golden; or use a combination), drained
Lemon wedges
Parsley sprigs

Instructions:
In a small bowl, combine chopped eggs and mayonnaise. Spread evenly on the bottom of a well-greased (I use butter), 9-inch springform pan. Cover eggs with chopped onion. In a small bowl, blend cream cheese with sour cream until smooth. Using a spatula, spread cream cheese mixture over the onion. Cover with plastic wrap and chill at least 3 hours or overnight. Before serving, top with drained caviar (Drain on paper towel and some will stick to the paper towel so you can lightly scrape them off). Now run a knife around the edge of the springform pan then unlatch and remove side.  Place the base on a large serving plate. Garnish with lemon wedges and parsley. Serve with unsalted crackers or pumpernickel bread
