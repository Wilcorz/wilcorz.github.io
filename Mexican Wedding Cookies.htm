Mexican Wedding Cookies

 

1 Cup Butter (soften over night)

½ Cup Powder Sugar

2 Cups Flour (all Purpose)

2 Cups Chopped Pecans (Finely Chopped)

1 Teaspoon Vanilla

1 Tablespoon Water

¼ Teaspoon Salt

 

Mix Butter, Powder Sugar, Vanilla, Water and Salt together.  Slowly mix in Flour and then Pecans.

 

Roll into bite size (smallest melon baller) and place on TWO lightly buttered cookie sheet about ½ inches apart.  Refrigerate for 45 minutes. 

 

Preheat oven to 270 degrees and bake for 45 minutes. DO NOT BROWN.

 

Remove from oven and immediately dust with powdered sugar in a bowl.

 

Remove from powdered sugar using a fork and place on a COOLING RACK.

 

These FREEZE very well.
