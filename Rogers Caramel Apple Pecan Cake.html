Roger's Caramel Apple Pecan Cake
Source: Roger
IngredientsInstructions

1 Cup Chopped NUTS

1 – 18 ½ oz Moist Yellow Cake Mix

1 – 3 ¾ oz. Instant French Vanilla pudding

4 – Eggs

1 Cup Water

1/3 Cup Wesson Vegetable Oil

3 - Fuji Apples, medium (Peeled, cored and chopped)

Topping:

30 - Caramels (unwrapped)

1 - Cup Chopped Pecans

1 Tablespoon Milk (add more if needed)

 Preheat oven to 350.

Spray bundt cake pan with “PAM For Baking with FLOUR”. 

Mix all ingredients together with a mixer until well mixed. Pour batter into bundt pan.

Bake for 1 hour. Remove from oven and let cool 20 minutes.  Invert onto plate. 

TOPPING:

Place Caramels, Pecan's and Milk in a microwave safe bowl or measuring cup.  Heat on high for 45 seconds.  Remove and stir.  Return to microwave for 45 seconds. Remove and stir until well blended.

Spoon hot topping mixture over cake and put in frig for 1 hour..  Serve and enjoy.
