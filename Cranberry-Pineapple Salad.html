Cranberry-Pineapple Salad

Source: Pam Currie

Ingredients

Instructions

1 can (8 oz.) Crushed Pineapple, drained reserving juice
1/2 Cup Ocean Spray Cranberry Juice
2 Tbsp. Lemon juice
1 - 3oz pkg Raspberry Gelatin
1 can (16 oz.) whole berry cranberry sauce 
1/2 cup chopped celery

1 Cup Chopped Pecans
 

Combine reserved pineapple juice, cranberry juice and lemon juice in a medium sauce pan, bring to a boil.  Remove from heat. Add gelatin stirring until completely dissolved.  Break up cranberry sauce with a fork.  Stir in geltain mixture.
REFRIGERATE until mixture begins to set.  Stir in Pineapple and celery (or pecans).  Pour into 4 cup mold and chill till firm.
