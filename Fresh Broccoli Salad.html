Fresh Broccoli Salad

 
IngredientsInstructions
4 cups fresh broccoli, coarsely chopped
1 red onion, finely chopped
1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
10 slices crisp bacon, crumbled

DRESSING:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sugar
4 Tblsp red wine vinegar
 

 Toss all salad ingredients. Mix dressing and pour over vegetables. Chill.
