Roger's Hearts of Palm Salad

Source: Roger
IngredientsInstructions

2 (14.8-ounce) cans of Artichoke Hearts Quartered
2 (14.8-ounce) jars or cans hearts of palm (Sliced)
2 (14.8-ounce) jars or cans Garbanzo Beans
1 (14.8-ounce) can of Sliced Black Olives
1 large Vidalia Onion (chopped)
Salt & Pepper to taste

DRESSING:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
 

Rinse and drain hearts of palm and garbanzo beans. If your hearts of palm are not already cut then diagonally cut into 3/4-inch-thick slices. 

Drain black olives and artichoke hearts (DO NOT RINSE).

Chop the Vidalia onion.

Toss all the above in a large bowl.

Dressing:
In a bowl, whisk together mayonnaise and vinegar until smooth. 

Toss salad to coat with dressing and season with salt and pepper. It can be made three days ahead of time and is best served cold.

Enjoy!
