Cake Mix Cookies

 

Marlene Gillio

 

1 (18.25 oz) package Devil Food cake mix

1 egg

½ (8oz.) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

1 cup chopped pecans

½ cup powder sugar

 

Combine first 3 ingredients, stirring well (dough will be sticky). Stir in shopped pecans.  Dust hands with powder sugar, and shape into ¾-inch balls.  Coat balls with powder sugar, and place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets.

 

Bake 350 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes or until done; remove to wire racks to cool.

 

5 - dozen
