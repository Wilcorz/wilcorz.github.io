PICKLED GREEN BEANS

PREP 45 minutes CHILL 4 hours

INGREDIENTS

3 to 3 ½ lbs. green beans and/or yellow wax beans

4 Cups Water

4 Cups Cider Vinegar

1 ½ TBSP. pickling slat

3 to 4 TBSP. Snipped Fresh Dill

¾ tsp. cayenne pepper

8 cloves garlic, sliced

PREPARATION 

1)     Wash beans; drain. Trim ends, if desired. Place beans in 8-quart Dutch oven. Add BOILING water to cover. Return to boiling. Cook uncovered, 3 minutes; drain. Place beans in large bowl.

2)     In Large saucepan combine the 4 cups water, vinegar, pickling salt, dill, cayenne pepper, and garlic. Bring to boiling. Pour over beans in bowl. Cool to room temperature. Transfer to glass container. Cover; chill at least 4 hours before serving.

3)     Store in refrigerator up to 2 weeks,
