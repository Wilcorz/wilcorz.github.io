Spicy Hot Crackers

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/4 cups canola oil
  • 1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
  • 2 tablespoons red pepper flakes
  • 1 (16 ounce) box saltine crackers

Directions:

  1. I use a larger pickle jar or a  large 2 gallon ziplock storage bag. Add the oil, ranch dressing mix, red pepper flakes together and mix until blended. Place crackers in container and pour mixture over crackers. Tumble constantly for 15 minutes.
  2. I also baked mine in the oven 250 for 20 minutes just because I like the toasty crunch that baking them gives.
     



Aunt Christa's Crackers

1 box saltines
1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
1/2 Teaspoon Garlic Salt
1/2 teaspoon Cayenne Pepper
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1 teaspoon Onion Powder
3 Tablespoons Ranch Dressing Powder
1 cup Olive Oil

Place crackers into Large Pickle Jar.  Mix ingredients together and pour over crackers.  Put lid on and rotate all directions until absorbed by crackers.

You can also baked mine in the oven 250 for 20 minutes just because I like the toasty crunch that baking them gives.
