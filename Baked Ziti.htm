Baked Ziti

 

1 and 1/2 - Boxes of Ziti (cooked and drained)

2 - jar of sauce (flavor you like)

1 lb. of mushroom (cooked and cooled)

3 - eggs

1 - large Ricotta

1/8 - Teaspoon of Oregano (may add more after tasting)

1 - large bag of mozzarella cheese (sliced or shred)

Parmesan cheese for the top.

 

In a bowl mix ricotta, salt, pepper and Oregano to taste (Be sure to taste before adding the eggs). Next add the 3 eggs, mixing well.

 

Now begin layering in large oblong pan. First the Sauce, then ziti, then ricotta mixture and then mozzarella cheese (continue this until all is gone) Sprinkle Parmesan & mozzarella cheese on top.


Cover loosely with foil and uncover 15 minutes before serving.

 

Bake at 375 till the cheese is melted.

 

You can make this up the day before and let it sit over night in the refrigerator..
