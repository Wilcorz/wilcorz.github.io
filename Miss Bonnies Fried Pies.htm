Fried Pie Crust

 

1 can pet milk

2 eggs

1 cup oil
2 tablespoon sugar

1 tsp vanilla

2 tablespoon salt

2 tablespoon baking powder

5 cups all purpose flour

 

Hand work in bowl.  Pinch in size desired and roll out. (Medium plate for template)

15+

 

Apple filling:

 

Cook 8 apples with ¼ cup water and drain off when cooked then add the following

1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice or apple pie spice – adjust to taste

1 cup sugar – adjust to taste

½ stick Butter

 

Thaw out for 10 minutes then cook on low in iron skillet. Flour test heat.

Brown on each side. Paper towel to remove excess oil.
