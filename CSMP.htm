Creamy Twice-Baked Maple Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe

Ingredients

  • .10 medium sweet potatoes  

  •  

  • 10 ounces cream cheese, softened  

  •  

  • .5 tablespoon brown sugar  

  •  

  • .1 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 

  •  

  • .1 cup TOASTED chopped pecans

  •   

  • .1/2 cup REAL Maple Syrup

  •   

  • .Mini-Marshmallows (or a crumble topping)  

  •  

Directions

  • .1. Scrub and pierce sweet potatoes. 

    Place on a cookie sheet lined with foil. 

    Bake at 375° for 1hr and 15 minutes or until tender.

    When cool enough to handle, cut a thin slice off the top of each potato and discard. Scoop out the pulp.      

 

  • .2. In a medium bowl, mash the pulp with cream cheese. 

    Stir in brown sugar, cinnamon and pecans.
     
    Spoon into a large casserole dish.
      
    Place on a baking sheet.      

 

  • .3. Bake, uncovered, at 375 degree for 45 minutes or until heated through.
     
    Remove from oven. 

 

  • .4. Score top in a grid pattern with a butter knife.  

    Drizzle Maple Syrup over top and into the grid lines.  

    Cover with Miniature Marshmallows.       

 

  • .5. Return to oven and set on Hi/Broil and brown Marshmallows to your liking. 
     
    Remove from oven and enjoy      

 

To reduce the amount divide the ingredient's by 5…
