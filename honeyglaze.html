Two Honey Glaze For Baked Ham
Source: RDW
Honey MustardHoney Orange
1/4 Cup Honey
1/2 Teaspoon dry mustard
1/4 Teaspoon ground cloves.
Mix honey, mustard and ground cloves together.
During the last hour of cooking the ham must be
basted every 15 minutes with this glaze.

1 can (6oz) frozen orange juice concentrate
1/2 Cup honey
1/2 Teaspoon ground ginger

Heat orange juice, honey and ground ginger to a boil,
stirring constantly.
Boil and stir 3 minutes.
During the last hour of cooking the ham must be
basted every 15 minutes with this glaze.
