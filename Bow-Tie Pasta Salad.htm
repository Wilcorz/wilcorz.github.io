Bow-Tie Pasta Salad (Renal/Diabetic Friendly!) 
Submitted by: Dirck Dimmick 

Ingredients:
- 1 lb. cooked bow-tie pasta (boiling salted water w/a dollop of olive oil)
- 1 cup celery (don't forget the leaves!), chopped
- 1 red bell pepper, chopped
- 1 carrot, shredded
- 1/2 lrg. purple onion, finely chopped
- 1/2 tsp. ground pepper
- 1 3/4 cups mayonnaise (I'm being generous on this;  less if you prefer)
- 1 1/2 tsps. sugar
- Juice of one lemon
 
Preparation:
 
1.  Mix pasta, celery, red bell pepper, carrot & purple onion in a large bowl. 
2.  In a separate small bowl, prepare dressing: blend pepper, mayonnaise, sugar and lemon juice until smooth.
3.  Pour dressing mixture over pasta and vegetables and mix until well coated. 
4.  Chill and serve.
 
Nutrients per serving (1/2 cup):
- Calories: 302
- Protein: 3 g.
- Carbohydrates: 19 g.
- Fat: 24 g.
- Cholesterol: 28 mg.
- Sodium: 178 mg.
- Potassium: 98 mg.
- Calcium: 13 mg.
- Fiber: 1 g.
 
Renal/Diabetic Food Choices:
- 1 1/2 starch
- 1/2 vegetable
