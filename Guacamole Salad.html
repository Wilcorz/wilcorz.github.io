Guacamole Salad
 
IngredientsInstructions

3 avocados, pureed (shells reserved)
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
Dash Tabasco sauce
2 Tblsp lemon juice
1 medium tomato, peeled & chopped
1/3 cup green onions, sliced
1/3 cup celery, sliced
1/2 green pepper, diced
 

 Bland avocado puree with mayonnaise, seasonings, and lemon juice. Fold in remaining ingredients, chill thoroughly. To serve, pile into avocado shells and garnish with crisp greens.
