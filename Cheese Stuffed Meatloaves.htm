Cheese Stuffed Meatloaves

 

Ingredients:

 

Cooking Spray

1 ½ Cups pasta sauce, divided

½ cup Italian bread crumbs

1 egg

½ chopped onions

1 ¼ lb lean ground beef, 7% fat

4 sticks Mozzarella String Cheese

 

Steps:

 

1)      Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Coat baking sheet with cooking spray. Combine ¾ cup pasta sauce and reaming ingredients (except cheese) until blended.

2)      Divide into four portions; shape around each string cheese, covering cheese completely. Seal tightly so hot cheese will stay inside.  Bake 20-25 minutes or until 160 degree.

3)      Before serving, microwave remaining ¾ cup pasta sauce on HIGH for 1 minute or until hot and spoon on top of each meatloaf patty.
