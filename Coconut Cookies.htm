Coconut Cookies

 

Preheat oven to 350

 

1 box yellow cake mix

2 cups shredded Coconut

1/4 Cup CoCo Lopez Cream of Coconut

1/4 cup soften butter

2 eggs

 

Stir all ingredients together. Roll into balls and roll in additional Coconut. Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake for 10 to 12 minutes.

 

3 - Dozen
