Grilled Chicken With Herbs
Susie Jacobs
IngredientsInstructions
3 lb Chicken, quartered
1/2 Cup Finely Chopped Parsley
1/3 Cup finely chopped fresh Fennel
1/2 Cup finely chopped Green Onions use white and pale green part (Scallions)
2 Tablespoons Plain Yogurt, Sour Cream or Creme Fraiche
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 Teaspoon Olive Oil		 Combine parsley, fennel, scallions, yogurt, salt, pepper and Olive oil in a bowl, so they are evenly mixed. Push fingers between the skin and the flesh to make a cavity right across each portion of chicken. Scoop up about one-fourth of the herb mixture in your fingers and shove under the chicken skin, pushing it gently until all parts of the flesh are covered. Massage the chicken to get the herbs evvenly distributed under the skni. Put the chicken aside for a good 30 minutes - the longer the better, even over night.
Grill or broil for about 30 minutes, turning once, until the chicken is brown and the juices run clear when the flesh is pierced.
