Arlene’s Beer Shrimp

 

1 lb uncooked Shrimp

3 cans of Beer

1 Tbs. mustard seed

1 Tbs. red pepper flakes

1 Tbs. Old Bay Seasoning

½ Tbs. celery salt

1 Cup of white vinegar

½ Cup Salt

 

Bring to boil and let boil for 5 minutes. 

 

Put in shrimp and return to boil.  Cook for about 5 minutes.  Drain, cool and serve with sauce below.

 

Cocktail sauce and ½ Tbs. horseradish
