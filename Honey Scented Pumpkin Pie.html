Honey Scented Pumpkin Pie

1 (15oz) can 100% pure Pumpkin Puree

1 cup Evaporated Milk

2 large Eggs

1/4 Cup Honey

1 1/2 teaspoons ground Cinnamon

1/2 teaspoon Allspice

1/4 teaspoon ground Cloves

2 Tablespoons Dark Brown Sugar

1 - (9-inch) ready-to-bake pie crust

Steps

1. Preheat oven to 375Â°

2. In a large bowl, whisk together pumpkin, evaporated milk, eggs, honey, cinnamon, allspice, cloves and brown sugar until well blended.

3. Pour pumpkin mixture into pie crust

4. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.

5. Cool completely on wire rack before slicing.  Store in frig.
