Grandpa Vic Lagorio’s Biscotti

 

Marlene Gillio

 

1 stick butter

1 1/2 cup sugar

4 eggs

2 teaspoon almond flavoring or Vanilla

4 cups Flour

2 teaspoon Baking Powder

1 Cup chopped nuts

 

Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs one at a time and flavoring. Add flour and baking powder. Mix well. Batter will be stiff.  Add nuts. Divide dough into 4 equal parts and spread into long strips on pam sprayed cookie sheets. Do not place to close as the dough will spread. Bake 350 degree until lightly browned. Take out and cut into 1 inch strips. Turn on their sides and return to oven and bake again until browned. Should be watched rather than timed. Makes several dozen.
