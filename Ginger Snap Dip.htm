Ginger Snap Dip
Source:
IngredientsInstructions
1 8 oz pkg Cream Cheese (Room temperature)
1 Cup Powder Sugar
2 Teaspoon Pumpkin Pie Spice
1 8 oz Cool Whip

1 16 oz Ginger Snap Cookies (store bought)
In a small mixing bowl, combine the cream cheese,
powdered sugar and pumpkin pie spice.
Beat in cool whip until blended.
Refrigerate until serving time.

Serve with gingersnaps for dipping.
As a fall dish, try serving the dip in a clean plastic pumpkin
