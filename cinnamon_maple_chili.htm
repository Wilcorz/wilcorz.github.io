Cinnamon Maple Chili

 

 

2 lbs ground chuck or ground turkey

½ roll of Jimmy Dean Maple Sausage

1 large can diced tomatoes

2 cans chili beans

1 can dark kidney beans

1 small can tomato sauce

2 cans corn

 

2 tablespoon chili powder

¼ tsp Cheyenne pepper (red)

1 teaspoon Cinnamon

1/2 teaspoon maple syrup

 

Brown beef and sausage in a large pot – do not drain

 

Add all canned items – do not drain any liquids from cans

 

Bring to slow boil

 

Keep stirring – do not allow chili to burn to bottom of pot.

 

Add chili powder, cayenne pepper, cinnamon and maple syrup .

 

Reduce heat, cover and simmer for a few hours.

 

Serve with toppings of Sour Cream and cheddar cheese.
